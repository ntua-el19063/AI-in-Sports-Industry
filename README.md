# AI-in-Sports-Industry

This repository contains an academic research project exploring the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) within the professional basketball industry. The work examines how AI-driven systems are currently being used by teams and organizations, and how these technologies influence training, performance, injury prevention, scouting, fan engagement, and the competitive environment of the sport.

## Purpose

The purpose of this study is to analyze the opportunities and challenges introduced by AI applications in the professional basketball industry, assessing both operational improvements and strategic implications. This project is relevant for roles in Business Intelligence, Data Strategy, and AI Consulting, as it demonstrates analytical research, industry evaluation, and technology impact assessment.

## Main Contents

- Research Paper (PDF / DOCX): Full academic report (~2500 words) analyzing AI applications in basketball.

- Industry Analysis: Application of Porter’s Five Forces to evaluate AI’s impact on the competitive landscape.

- Case Study: Review of Golden State Warriors’ use of AI-driven training technologies.

- Literature Review & References: Collection of academic and industry sources supporting the study.

## Key Work Conducted

- Investigated current applications of AI in coaching, training systems, injury prevention, and fan experience.

- Analyzed risks and concerns, including data privacy, overdependence on automation, and employment displacement.

- Evaluated strategic outcomes using business analysis frameworks.

- Identified expected future trends and developments leading up to 2030.
